About y33tsp00l

y33tsp00l is an exploit created by Wicorn29 with the goal of being the successor to ExtHang3r. The exploit recreates the behavior of the LTMEAT Print method by flooding iframes and then printing the page. For whatever reason, printing a page with a ton of iframes hangs the embedded page rather than the host page. This is exactly what y33tsp00l exploits, since it also works with extension pages (as long as they are under web_accessible_resources). This seems to be much more consistent than other extension-freezing methods, and it also lasts for a longer period of time.
